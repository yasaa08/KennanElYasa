About

Hi, I'm Kennan

I’m Kennan — a Bandung-based developer and Software Engineering student interested in the intersection of code and human-centric solutions. What started in vocational school evolved into a full-blown obsession with understanding how apps work, from the core logic to the final pixel.


Lately, I’ve been building with Flutter and Web technologies. My latest project, 'Kita 46.2', is an all-in-one platform built to replace heavy physical books during worship, making spiritual practice more accessible. When I’m not coding, I’m probably swimming, gaming, or cycling around the city.


Former student leader turned programmer. I’m focused on building things that are clear, functional, and designed to make life easier.
